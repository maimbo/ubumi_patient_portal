Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I update my personal information?

A: You can update your personal information by navigating to the 'Profile' page from the user menu in the top right corner.

Q: Is my medical data secure?

A: Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: You can reset your password by clicking the 'Forgot Password?' link on the login page.

Q: How can I book an appointment?

A: Navigate to the 'Appointments' section from the sidebar and click on 'Book New Appointment'. Follow the on-screen instructions.

Q: Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

A: Yes, you can cancel or reschedule appointments from the 'Appointments' page. Please note any cancellation policies that may apply.

Q: How do I view my bills?

A: You can view your past and current bills in the 'Billing' section, accessible from the sidebar.

Q: Is Mapipo Health integrated with NHIMA?

A: Yes, Mapipo Health supports NHIMA. You can add your NHIMA details in your profile for seamless billing.

Q: Who do I contact for billing queries?

A: For any billing-related questions, please contact our support team through the 'Support' page or call our helpline.
